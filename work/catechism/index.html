Luther's Small Catechism App

Overview

Overview

Conveniently reference the official 1986 version of Luther’s Small Catechism on the go, from phone to tablet to PC. This beautifully designed app makes reading and navigating the Small Catechism easy and enjoyable.

View App

My Role

  • Prototype
  • UI Design

Optimized for Legibiltiy

Special care went into selecting typography, the final selection balancing readability with reverence for the history of the text.

Faster Scrolling

With the addition of the Explanation to the Catechism, a new way to scroll through content was necessary. Sections were numbered to make it easier to find needed information, as well as a jump-to-scroll tool to make the even process faster.

Multiple Translations

Font support for eight different languages was added to the app, paving the way for increased global use in the future.
