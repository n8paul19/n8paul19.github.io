Overview

A healthcare product that empowers employees to select the best plan for their needs or for companies to improve their employees’ understanding of how to choose and use their benefits. Needed help transforming their user experience to be more competitive. Emphasis was made on responsive design and inclusive design.

Hub Page

Created a Hub page where people start, return and finish their tasks. The challenge her was to provide as much information to the user on one screen without overwheling them with too much. An uncluttered UI was essential to the success of the product.

Responsive Design and Accessible

The new experience had to look great on any device. Special attention was poured into the UI to meet AAA color contrast standards.

Make it even easier

The new design had to make the experience of choosing your healthcare plan an easy and fun experience. Educational prompts help those users that needed just a little more information.
