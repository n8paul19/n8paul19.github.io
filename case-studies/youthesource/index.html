Case Study YouthESource

Redeveloping YouthESource for a Modern, Engaging Experience

Overview

YouthESource.com is a Wordpress platform dedicated to providing valuable resources and guidance to youth leaders and adults. The goal of this project was to redesign the site for a modern, mobile-first experience, upgrade it to the latest version of WordPress, and enhance its performance, security, and content discoverability.
