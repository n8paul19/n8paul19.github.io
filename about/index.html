About Me

I'm Nate Paul, an ecommerce manager and multidisciplinary designer with over 20 years of experience shaping user-focused digital products. I lead design, UX strategy, and front-end direction for enterpise level ecommerce experiences—working closely with product, engineering, and marketing to create interfaces that are intuitive, scalable and meaningful.

My approach blends systems thinking, hands-on design, and a calm, collaborative leadership style. I love mentoring others, improving processes, and strengthening the connection between good design and real business outcomes.

My faith in Christ guides how I work and how I lead, and my family is the foundation that keeps me grounded and motivated.
