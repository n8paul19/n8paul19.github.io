A little about us

Invelop LLC is synonymous with the highest quality visual solutions for residential and commercial spaces.

Invelop LLC is the local pioneer of 3-dimensional showcases and visual tours within the St. Louis Region, and combining this technology with high-definition, low-altitude aerial imagery, Invelop LLC is the premier choice for the residential and business clients that need or want to render their desired locations into 3-dimensional visual models.

The photography and image processing prowess from CDM Productions is combined with the marketing and sales knowledge of the CFB Team, thus leaving the marketplace with only clear choice for 3-dimensional productions.

It is no secret that there are other providers of imagery solutions to the residential and commercial marketplace, but we have yet to see any presentation of residential or commercial property in the St. Louis Region that truly reflects the condition, appeal, and features of the subject in addition to allowing a consumer to travel (visually) to any area within the 3-dimensional showcase immediately upon desire. Plainly, the existing providers in the St. Louis Region are creating cheap, low-resolution, low-quality, and stitched-together videos (made from still photographs) that do not correlate with the level of technology that is available to these particular vendors. In addition, it does not appear that any of the providers are using professional editing, thus further lowering the quality of their deliverable product.
