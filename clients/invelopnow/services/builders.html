Builders

This technology is an ideal tool for the construction industry and it can benefit nearly any builder and/or construction company. 3D showcases open up a new range of possibilities for communicating with their clients and increasing new sales. Construction managers can quickly perform a 3D showcase to share project progress with clients and colleagues. Clients that are not locally available can utilize their builder's 3D showcase to make informed decisions relating to design, project status, and any purchasing decisions that require the client's visual interaction. Sub-contractors and team members can check on project status and have a much better idea of what the actual schedule is.

Builders' 3D showcases are certainly not limited to residential construction. In fact, this technology yields a higher benefit to commercial builders due to the necessity to maintain a transparent project status, a cohesive team and a clear communication regimen regarding issues and project status with clients who are most often located outside of the project's geographic location. Multiple scans can be performed over the life of the project keeping the entire team on the same page with respect to the project's progress and visualization of the overall space as it is created.
