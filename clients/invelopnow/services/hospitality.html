Hospitality

There has never been a better time than now for this technology to become available. Luxury spending is the highest it has ever been, travel is easier than ever, and competition for local and international travelers is extreme. Being able to present a transparent viewpoint of a resort, a hotel, or an accommodation should put any guest at ease by offering an immersive experience before the guest physically arrives. There is no better way to present an area or a locale to the world than to allow for potential guests to experience an immersive, self-guided tour of the premises via a 3D showcase. Our team can rapidly scan a large area and have the showcase available for viewing within a matter of hours. A 3D showcase eliminates any complicated photography and videography sessions, photo/video review and selection process, and many other visual marketing options that are included in traditional marketing packages. Not only is the 3D showcase available within a matter of hours, but any number of high-quality digital photographs can be captured from the showcase and used separately in alternate marketing pieces. In addition, a guided walk-through video can also be created directly from the 3D showcase. The combination of features, the affordability of the service, and the time that is necessary to complete the above leads the hospitality industry to a very easy and singular decision.
