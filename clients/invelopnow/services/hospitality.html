Hospitality

Luxury spending is the highest it has ever been, travel is easier than ever, and competition for local and international travelers is extreme. There has never been a better time than now for this technology to become available to the Hospitality Industry.

Being able to present a transparent viewpoint of a resort, a hotel, or an accommodation should put any guest at ease by offering an immersive experience before the guest physically arrives. There is no better way to present an area or a locale to the world than to allow for potential guests to experience an immersive, self-guided tour of the premises via a 3D Showcase.

A 3D Showcase can eliminate complicated photo/video sessions, selection processes, and many other visual marketing options that are present in traditional marketing packages. Our team can rapidly scan a large area and have a 3D Showcase available for viewing within a matter of hours. Any number of high-quality digital photographs can be captured from the showcase and used separately in alternate marketing pieces. In addition, a guided walk-through video can also be created directly from the 3D Showcase. The combination of features, the affordability of the service, and the time that is necessary to complete the above is a powerful tool for the Hospitality Industry.
