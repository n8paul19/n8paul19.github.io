Property Managers

With respect to leasing and managing properties, following maintenance one of the largest expenditures of time and energy is related to showing potential tenants what options are available for lease.

Once a 3D showcase is performed on a single family house, condominium, loft, or apartment, this 3D showcase can be perpetually stored and turned on whenever the need arises to market that unit - regardless of its occupancy and/or rehabilitation status. One full scan of a unit will last as long as the digital data will (forever) and can be viewed an infinite number of times. A 3D showcase will remove the necessity to either locate an empty unit, show an unit that is occupied, or lease an unit that cannot be toured/viewed for a variety of reasons. A 3D showcase increases the manager's ability to market and lease any unit at any time of the year.

This technology can be leveraged in order to eliminate any unnecessary time gap between getting an unit ready for lease and re-leasing the unit, thus saving either the management company a penalty or saving the property's owner from additional fees and/or debt service not being supplemented by rental funds. The ability to digitally archive a showcase and turn it on at will is an immense advantage over the competition and provides any property management company a critical separation from competition.

3D showcases are certainly not limited to residential property management companies.

Commercial property managers can utilize 3D showcases to allow possible lessees to visualize their corporation's options without having to include costly travel expenses and multiple floor-plan modeling into their budgets. 3D showcases can be applied to virtually any size commercial space. By including additional digital processing* into the showcase(s), scanned areas can be manipulated to the point where a commercial space can have any number of items digitally inserted into the area, as many times as desired, so the potential lessee can explore multiple floor plan/build-out options without having to bear the expense of traditional architectural modeling. The inclusion of this technology into the marketing portfolio of Commercial Property Managers is a revolutionary step in 21st century marketing.
