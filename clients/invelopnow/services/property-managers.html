Property Managers

One of the largest expenditures of time and energy is related to showing potential clients what options are available for lease.

Commercial: Property managers can utilize 3D Showcases to allow possible lessees to visualize their options without having to include costly travel expenses and multiple floor-plan modeling into their budgets. 3D Showcases can be applied to virtually any size commercial space. The 3D Showcase creates digital files that can be shared with architects, providing them with the exact spacial dimensions they require to create multiple floor plan/build-out options without having to bear the expense of traditional architectural modeling. The inclusion of this technology into the marketing portfolio of Commercial Property Managers is a revolutionary step in 21st century marketing.

Residential: Once a 3D Showcase is performed on an apartment, condominium, loft, or single family house, a 3D Showcase can be perpetually stored and turned on whenever the need arises to market that unit - regardless of its occupancy and/or rehabilitation status. One full scan of a unit will last as long as digital data (forever) and can be viewed an infinite number of times. A 3D Showcase will remove the necessity to either locate an empty unit, show a unit that is occupied, or lease a unit that cannot be toured/viewed for any reason. A 3D Showcase increases the manager's ability to market and lease any unit at any time of the year.

This technology can eliminate any unnecessary time gap between getting a unit ready for lease and re-leasing the unit, thus saving the management company marketing expenses and saving the property's owner from additional fees and/or debt service not being supplemented by rental funds. The ability to digitally archive a 3D Showcase and turn it on at will is an immense advantage over the competition and provides any property management company critical separation from competition.
